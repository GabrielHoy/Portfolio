Why Choose Me Instead of Someone Else?

Familiarity

I'm familiar with many different language styles, from lower level C-style langages to web languages like JS, HTML and CSS to Python and Lua, i'm comfortable writing in many different things.

Clean Design

I often find wild code to be either messy and undocumented or packaged with a wall of text attempting to explain everything at the start. I make sure all my variable names are intuitive and my scripts are readable from the start to increase the productivity of both me and future programmers.

Speed

I like to consider myself a "fast" programmer, and I am confident that many of my references will agree with this opinion. Often I pride myself with the speed and efficiency at which I code compared to other people.

Teamwork

I have always held a special place in my heart for programming and the development process. I am extremely self driven and more than happy to work with any team involved, and have past experience forming and cooperating in teams on hobby projects.

Lua|LuaU
6 Years hobby experience

I've been programming in Lua and LuaU for a long time. At around the age of 10 I was a player on the "ROBLOX" gaming platform and liked the games, so I decided to try and make them myself in their LuaU language, a modification of Lua. Little did I know, this freedom would spark a giant interest in code for years to come, and develop me personally and professionally massively, in addition to me doing both freelance and team-based work on the platform, preparing me more than I could ever want for a "real" job.

C++
2 Years hobby experience, 1 Year education

After a long time on the ROBLOX platform, I discovered it had a backend in C++ and was introduced to the wonderful world of low-level programming. This opened many doors for me due to the limitations on what exactly you can do in a Lua environment sometimes and I thrived immediately, learning the syntax and usage of C++ and continuing to pick up and learn about popular and industry standard libraries like Boost, FMOD, OpenGL and supporting libraries like GLFW/GLEW/GLM. This would go to help me massively in my Computer Software Development class in which I would use C++ for my projects. I enjoy working in C++ very much and visit the language to keep up to scratch very often(as much as a hobbyist teenager can) , and would consider myself proficient in the language.
